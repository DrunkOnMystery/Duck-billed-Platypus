The duck-billed platypus: evolution's most curious creature

The duck-billed platypus is one of the strangest creatures on Earth. A native of the Australian continent, the platypus is a mammal with the bill of a duck, poisoned spines, and it lays eggs like a bird.

Adorably cute, despite being dangerous, this curious creature is a fascinating look at how evolution can and has ended up on truly marvelous, strange creatures.
